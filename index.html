Process

I use Everett McKay's Design Process, detailed in UI is communication, as a framework for design. Using this as a guideline allows me to focus less on process on more on the project. This is definitely iterative and I revisit areas as the project dictates.

Plan

What

We start with “WHAT”. What's the product concept and vision? I sometimes think about this as an insight or idea.

Who

Next step in my process is “WHO”. Who are the users? What are their goals, tasks, and problems? In some ways, we are validating that the initial “WHAT” is an actual problem for the users.

How

Next step in my process is “How”. How do we we stay focused on our what and how? Basically, consider and plan for constraints, but also define a direction and a theme.

Design

Ideate

In the first ideate phase we are trying to come up with as many ideas as possible by brainstorming and sketching

Select

I select an idea to go forward with

Ideate

I conduct a second round of ideation in order to diverge.

Choose

I choose the idea thats most promising

Refine

Present

Test

Here is an opportunity to test it with users.

Iterate

Now to further iterate on the feedback.
