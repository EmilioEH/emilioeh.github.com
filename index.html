About

Hi!

I'm a designer that lives in Austin, TX. I'm actually from a small-ish town, Beaumont,TX. I've always had an interest in creative pursuits and thats manifested in different ways. As a kid i loved playing games. In middle school I discovered video production and would work on that on and off until about 2016. In high school I started a chess club. I was never good enough to compete but always had fun. While in high school I discovered the drums. This is what got me to Austin,TX. I was in a band and we all moved there. After some shows here and there, that ended. Soma random jobs and then I got into amateur graphic design. Then, I enrolled in Austin Community College for their UX program.

Once I discovered design I started to realize somethings I wanted to achieve in life. One of my best friends committed suicide and I decided to start to leverage my design skills to have impact. That's what I'm passionate about now. Creatively solving problems that help people.
