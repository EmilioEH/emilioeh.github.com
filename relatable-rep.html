Summary

Relatable Rep is an auto sales startup based in Florida that improves the car buying experience by allowing customers to choose their salesperson by personality profiling. Based on my work, I concluded that although the product worked for select users, it ran into multiple issues and that the product should pivot and find a different solution for improving the car buying experience.
