About

Hi!

I'm a UX designer who firmly believes in the power of collaboration (join my open source project). I'm extremely passionate about solving real problems (check out my work with NAMI), but I'm not all work. I also enjoy playing chess and jamming on the drums.

Key Skills:

  • Develop a practical and living project plan that balances user goals, business needs, and any constraints
  • Build and execute a research plan, synthesis research, and then present actionable findings
  • Use Research findings to generate design iterations then build and validate a prototype
  • Exercise leadership, collaborate, and encourage a team by listening and responding to their contributions
