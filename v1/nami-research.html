Preface

Overview

This project, an assignment for a UX Applied Lab class at Austin Community College, was different from other projects in one major way. This time there was a client, the National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI) Central Texas. This project allowed me to fully comprehend and practice the two types of UX research: Generative and evaluative. Generative is truly about exploring unknown space and learning. Evaluative is about determining if a design is successful in meeting the user’s goals.
