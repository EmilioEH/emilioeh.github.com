Case Studies

Here is a collection of case studies and current projects I'm working on now.

Lessons in Soft Skills

Client: NAMI Central, TX
Time Frame: Feb. - May 2019

I initially went into this project like other projects I’ve worked on at my job, HEB, but I quickly realized that business approach wasn’t the most effective. Because of the subjective nature of design I got a crash course in ‘soft’ skills.

Generative and Evaluative Research

Client: NAMI Central, TX
Time Frame: Feb. - May 2019

Through this project, I came to fully comprehend and practice the two types of UX research: Generative and evaluative.

From Mock-ups to Prototype

Client: Relatable Rep
Time Frame: Oct 2018 - Jan 2019

Based on my work, I concluded that although the product worked for select users, it ran into multiple issues and that the product should pivot and find a different solution for improving the car buying experience.

Get Out of the Building

Client: ACC - User Interface
Time Frame: April - May 2018

The biggest lesson, however, was that I hadn’t followed Steve Blank’s advice to “...get out of the building” to actually test. My final design only worked inside the building.
